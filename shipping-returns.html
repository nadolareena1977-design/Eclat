Shipping & Returns

Shipping Information

Free Worldwide Shipping

We offer complimentary shipping on all orders, regardless of your location or order value.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

Delivery Times

  • India: 3-5 business days
  • International: 7-14 business days
  • Remote Locations: 10-21 business days

Tracking

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships, allowing you to monitor delivery progress.

Returns & Refunds

30-Day Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for items in their original, unused condition with original packaging.

Return Process

  1. Contact us at hello@luxemug.com within 30 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll provide a return shipping label
  4. Package and ship the item back to us
  5. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Refund Information

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Return shipping costs are covered by Èclat for eligible returns.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items that have been used or damaged
  • Items without original packaging
  • Personalized or custom items
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective)

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost.
